About the eBook

"Unlocking Affordable Housing" demystifies the Low-Income Housing Tax Credit (LIHTC) program for developers, investors, and stakeholders seeking to create and preserve affordable housing. With the latest updates and regulations incorporated, alongside best practices and real-world case studies, this indispensable resource empowers readers to successfully leverage the LIHTC program and make a meaningful impact in addressing the affordable housing crisis.
